Whole-Grain Bread Recipe

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 450g whole-grain flour
  • 50g white flour
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 300ml warm water
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions

  1. Combine warm water, yeast, and honey. Let it activate for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in flours and salt. Knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour, then shape it into a loaf.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes at 200°C (390°F).
